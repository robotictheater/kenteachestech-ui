Is Your Child Interested In
Programming, Website Design, Video Game Development, 3D Printing, Electronics or Robotics?

I provide asyncronous private lessons that allow your child to learn at their own pace and on a schedule that fits your family.

Asyncronous does not mean alone! Help is only a click away. I am there to guide the student, answer questions, provide feedback, grade projects and make sure they get the most out of each lesson. As the parent, you get access to a dashboard to view your childs progress.

About Me

I have an education in electronics and software development, and have spent the past two decades building everything from websites to robots. Mobile apps to video games. Smart-devices to machine learning systems.

As homeschooling family, the past ten years has been spent with my wife (who is a state certified teacher) working together to educate our three children.

I now focus on developing fun and engaging technology coursework for children ages 10 to 18.

When not in front of my laptop, my favorite thing to do is spend time with my family.

Popular Paths

Think of "Paths" like college majors. While multiple paths may share a common "Intro to programming" starting point, as the student progresses the specific classes they take will be dependent on their interests. Below I have assembled some popular course paths that you could follow. However you are not limited to a pre-defined set of classes and choose your own path.
